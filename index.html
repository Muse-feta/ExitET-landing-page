✦ Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for your exit exams with ExitET.

What departments are covered in ExitET?

ExitET supports over 50 academic departments, covering main fields in Engineering, Business & Economics, Medicine & Health Sciences, Natural Sciences, and Social Sciences.

How do I download and install the app?

Simply tap the Download APK button in the hero section to save the file. Open it on your Android device and tap install. If prompted, make sure to allow installations from unknown sources in your device settings.

Is the app free to use?

Yes! ExitET is 100% free to download and use. You get full access to all questions, answers, and department feeds without any hidden fees or in-app purchases.

Can I practice without an internet connection?

Yes! Once you download your specific department's questions, you can access and practice them completely offline. An internet connection is only needed to load updates and participate in the community discussion board.
